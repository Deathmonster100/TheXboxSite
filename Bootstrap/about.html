About Xbox
By Pranav Sudheer, 2:30 AM 19 July, 2019


Xbox has been here for a long time, to be specific 18 years. The first Xbox was released 15 November, 2001 in North America. When they first released it they decided that they were going to call it the DirectX-box because of the software they used. From then there has been 6 systems released.

These systems are:

  • The original Xbox
  • Xbox 360
  • Xbox One
  • Xbox One S
  • Xbox Project Scorpio
  • Xbox Project Scarlet

These consoles (other than Project Scarlet because it didn't come out yet) sold in millions making billions for Microsoft. However, that wasn't always the case. In 2005, Microsoft reported that they lost $4 billion from the Xbox franchise. However, throughout the years the XBOX started making big bucks because of the upgrades in the softwares they introduced. In the end, the XBOX is one of the leading brands in the gaming industry.
